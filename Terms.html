  • Donors must be in good health and meet the eligibility criteria established by the blood donation center.
  • Donors must be at least 18 years old (or as required by local laws) and must provide valid identification at the time of donation.
  • Donors must weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs) unless specific exemptions are granted by medical personnel.
  • Certain medical conditions, recent travel, or other factors may disqualify you from donating. You agree to honestly answer all pre-donation screening questions.

2. Personal Information

  • By booking an appointment, you consent to the collection, use, and storage of your personal information as described in our Privacy Policy.

  • Your information will be used to determine eligibility, schedule appointments, and contact you regarding your donation.

3. Appointment Booking

  • Appointments are scheduled on a first-come, first-served basis. The time slot selected during the booking process will be reserved for you, subject to availability.
  • If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please do so as early as possible to allow others to book your slot.

4. Donation Process

  • All donors will undergo a pre-donation screening to ensure eligibility. This includes answering questions about your health and recent activities.
  • During the donation, you will be monitored by trained medical staff. Follow their instructions and inform them immediately if you feel unwell.

5. Health and Safety

  • Your health and safety are our top priorities. We follow strict hygiene and safety protocols to ensure a safe donation experience.
  • If you experience any adverse reactions after donation, please contact us immediately or seek medical attention.
