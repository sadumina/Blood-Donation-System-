Blood

Blood is a vital fluid that circulates through the human body, delivering essential nutrients and oxygen to cells while removing waste products. Composed of red blood cells, white blood cells, platelets, and plasma, it plays a crucial role in maintaining bodily functions and regulating temperature.

Blood banks

Blood banks are crucial institutions that play a vital role in healthcare worldwide. They serve as repositories for donated blood, ensuring a steady and safe supply for medical procedures, emergencies, and transfusions.

Donors

Blood donors are individuals who selflessly contribute their blood to blood banks and hospitals, playing a crucial role in saving lives.

Donor's past records

These records are typically kept confidential by healthcare providers or blood donation centers to ensure privacy and compliance with regulations.
